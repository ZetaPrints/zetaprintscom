Web-to-print service

Use the Web to Grow Your Printing Business

The ZetaPrints web-to-print service gives your printing business a quick and easy way to take customer orders online. Everything you (and your customers) need is available online 24/7 through the ZetaPrints website. Or, you can set it up so your customers can access a website bearing your company logo and name.

No need for them (or you) to invest in new hardware or software.

And the good news is that it’s affordable. In fact, the cost-per-order is probably less than what you’re paying to process a single order over the phone. No monthly or set-up fees, either. The cost-per-order is the only fee you pay to use the service.

More good news:

  • The system uses the same design files your customers are already using.
  • You or your designers can upload user document templates.
  • Templates are available as soon as someone uploads them into the system.
  • A job proof is available online within seconds.
  • You get instant e-mail notification of new orders.
  • You can track orders online and download print-ready files when ready.
  • All information about orders, your customers and your company is secure.
  • The system can be used for variable data, short run, and long run printing.
