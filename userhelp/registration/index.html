Registration

There are three different ways to get registered for online print ordering: self-registration, company registration and individual registration.

Self-registration

You can register your self for online print ordering. Go to http://demo.zetaprints.com/ and click on theÂ  “I want to register” link.

Watch a Flash demonstration on this topic

Enter your email address on the next page and click the GO button.

Fill in the registration form on the next page and click Register.

After your registration is processed you will receive an email confirming your registration. You can then access your account and the template catalogs.

Company registration

A printer can register you as a company so that all employees can self-register using their work email address. The system will recognize them by the domain part fo the address.

Individual registration

A printer can register you as an individual. You will receive a registration email with a link. Click on the link to confirm your registration and access your account.
