Industry solutions

Web-to-print is a versatile technology. We can help you whenever you need a template based PDF generation. That is, when you want non-designers to produce print ready files.

ZetaPrints is an open platform. It can be used by anyone in the supply chain of printed designs:


Printers– taking orders from customers
Publishers– online and print advertising booking
Designers– selling designs or simplifying design variations
Businesses– simplifying their print ordering and display advertising

Franchises– maintaining the brand and driving the costs down
Online advertisers– full or partial automation of banner generation
Online publishers– making banner advertising easy and cost effective for SME
Large businesses– taking control of your print back from print providers
