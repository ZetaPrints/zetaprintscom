Coming up stable web-to-print release for Magento

Web-to-print storefront
02/01/2012

We will be releasing the latest beta version of our web-to-print extension for Magento some time this week.

The release has important fixes. We strongly recommend upgrading, specially if you don’t have our new fancy image editor in your web-to-print site.

Don’t be shy to ask for help with the upgrade if you are an existing paying customer.

Open source web-to-print storefronts

Web-to-print storefront
30/08/2011

We are continuing to add to our collection of manuals for creating free open source storefronts for printing and graphic design businesses, with or withing web-to-print and dynamic imaging.

The most recent addition is an article on creating and sending out newsletters from within Magento. It is a relatively simple process anyone familiar with a rich text editor can master within an hour or two. You can always get a professional to do it for you, but having the knowledge and understanding of the process helps anyway. Enjoy.
