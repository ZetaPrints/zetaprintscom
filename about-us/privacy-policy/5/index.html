Privacy policy

We value your privacy

Zeta Prints Ltd has drafted this document, which details how we gather and disseminate information, in an effort to demonstrate our strong commitment to online privacy. Please contact us if you have any questions about our privacy policy.

Use of Registration Information

Zeta Prints Ltd collects only information required for the business process served by the website. Any identifying information can be potentially supplied to other users of the website according to the rules of the business process.

Use of Credit Card Details

Customer credit card details are never stored, cached or logged by us. Zeta Prints passes such details onto internet payment gateways for processing and may store transaction ID, authorization code and other details, not the credit card number, holder name or expiry date.

Information Disclosure

No personal or identifying information will be shared with any third party, except if
a) the third party is part of the normal business process
or
b) it is required by law

Logging and Information Retention

Any information provided by users, except for credit card details, can be logged and retained for an unspecified period of time for security and audit purposes.

Use of Demographic Information

No such information is collected.

Use of Order Information

When you place an order, Zeta Prints Ltd collects information such as contact information, and your name as part of the business process on behalf of the printing organisation. The contact information you provide is required to fill your orders, however, Zeta Prints Ltd should not be held liable for any misuse of information by the printing organisation.

Use of Financial Information

Your financial information is used for the sole purpose of billing you for products and services. This type of information can be shared with other computer systems within Zeta Prints Ltd for accounting purposes.

File uploading

You may upload different file types to the server as part of the normal business process. Zeta Prints Ltd reserves the right to check any such files for the purpose of security, site performance or other relevant matters. We may contact you regarding some of your files with suggestions (e.g. how to reduce a size of a template without sacrafising quality), warnings (e.g. if you upload inapproriate content) or for other relevant reasons.

We require that you hold all necessary copyright and licensing for the uploaded content and may request a proof if a particular breach is brought to our attention.

Security

We strive to maintain security at the highest level possible to protect the loss, misuse or alteration of information placed under our control.

Children’s Guidelines and Offensive Contents

Unsupervised children are not expected to be users of the website. However, it is possible that some templates may contain nudity or offensive language. Zeta Prints Ltd does not censor the content unless a particular case is brought to our attention.

Communications

Zeta Prints Ltd reserves the right to communicate to the users by email, phone, fax or post using the details provided on the website. There must be a genuine reason for such communication to happen, e.g. request of a lost password, billing issues, account update, changes in functionality, etc.

Correct/Update Personal Information

Zeta Prints Ltd allows you to change or modify your personal information within the boundaries of the business process. Any changes to personal information beyond that should be dealt with on a case by case basis.

Links to other sites

This site contains links to other sites. Zeta Prints Ltd is not responsible for the content or privacy practices of these sites.

Contact Us

If you have any additional questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please contact us.
